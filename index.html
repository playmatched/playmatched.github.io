How Points Work
IPL 2026 · Matched scoring
🏏
Batting Impact
(Player Runs ÷ Team Total Runs) × 100
60 in 120 scores more than 60 in 220.
🎯
Bowling Impact
(Player Wickets ÷ Total Wickets Fell) × 100
Every wicket counts more when fewer fall.
🏆
Win Bonus
+20 points if your player's team won.
📊
Total Score
Batting + Bowling + Win Bonus, cumulative across all IPL 2026 matches.
✅
No Negative Points
Bad match = 0 points. Never penalised.
